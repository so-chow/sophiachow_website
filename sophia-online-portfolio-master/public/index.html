about me

I'm a multi-talented individual skilled at and interested in teaching, writing, crafting, psychoanalysis, and music. I am currently working on embracing my artist self and hope to one day share my writing, poetry, and art to the world. I'm hoping to uplift others with my art and to shine a light on issues of education, emotional and physical abuse, women of color (speciaically as an asian american), and removing the stigma of mental health issues. I want people to know they aren't alone in their stuggles. I hope my art, my writing, my poetry, my journals, through sharing my struggles and successes will lift people up, light the way, and bring joy and empowerment to anyone out there who is working on embracing themselves and living up to their highest creative potential.
